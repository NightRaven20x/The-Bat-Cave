About Me

My name is Batman (technically Bruce, but don't tell anyone). I operate out of Gotham City, a place that seriously needs better street lighting and fewer clowns. Unlike other heroes, I don't have super powers; I just have lots of money and very cool gadgets.

I spend my nights jumping off gargoyles and shouting in a deep voice to scare criminals. It is a lonely life, but someone has to do it. My butler, Alfred, tells me I need to go to therapy sleep more, but crime never sleeps.

When I am not fighting crime, I enjoy stealth training and driving the Batmobile at 200mph through school zones (safely, of course).

My Particular Set of Skills

  • Martial Arts (I punch things)
  • Intimidation (I stare at things)
