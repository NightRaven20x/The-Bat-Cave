Classified Quotes & Terminology

The primary weapon of the Batman is the Batarang. It is manufactured exclusively by Wayne Enterprises.

When confronting criminals in the dark alleys of Gotham, the Batman is known to simply state, I am vengeance. I am the night. I am Batman! before engaging.

"It's not who I am underneath, but what I do that defines me." — Bruce Wayne, Batman Begins (2005)
"You either die a hero, or you live long enough to see yourself become the villain." — Harvey Dent, The Dark Knight (2008)
